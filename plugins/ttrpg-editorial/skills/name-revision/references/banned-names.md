## Banned Patterns

### Suffixes to Avoid
- Female: -ina, -ine, -ara, -yra, -iel, -ael, -wyn, -wen, -elle, -ette
- Male: -ian, -ius, -ael, -iel, -or, -yr, -ric (unless historical)
- Places: -mere, -hollow, -vale, -crest, -spire, -haven, -wood (as compound)

### Prefixes to Avoid
- Syl-, Lyr-, Cel-, Ser-, Ael-, Thal-, Mal- (dark/evil), Bel- (beautiful), Mor- (dark)
- Shadow-, Moon-, Star-, Storm-, Night-, Dark-, Raven-, Wolf-, Dragon-

### Compound Patterns to Avoid
- [Color] + [Animal/Weapon/Natural feature]
- [Time of day] + [Geographic feature]
- [Element] + [Suffix]
- [Abstract concept] + -bearer/-walker/-bringer/-seeker
