# Feat Effect Library — Reusable Mechanical Bricks

Official 2024 feats are assembled from a small vocabulary of recurring
mechanical bricks. Build custom feats from these bricks: **2–3 bricks
maximum per feat, exactly one of them the signature**. A feat whose
every brick exists elsewhere is bland; a feat whose every brick is novel
is unreadable. One new thing, anchored by familiar ones.

## Resource Patterns (the 2024 engines)

| Pattern | Expected usage | Official examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Once per turn** | Every round, self-limiting in nova turns | Savage Attacker, Great Weapon Master's rider |
| **Per Long Rest (1×)** | Once per adventuring day — story-beat sized | Magic Initiate's free cast |
| **Per Short or Long Rest** | 2–3× per day — encounter-sized | (common on class features; rare on feats — use sparingly) |
| **PB per Long Rest** | Scales 2 → 6 across the game — the 2024 default for countable resources | Lucky's Luck Points, Musician's Inspiration |
| **Recharge on Initiative** | Once per combat, flavor of readiness | Boon of Fate |

Pick the pattern by asking *how often should the table see this?* —
then never combine two resource patterns in one feat.

## Combat Bricks

**Attack riders** (resolve on hit, once per turn):
- Bonus damage: +PB damage on hit with [weapon class], once per turn
  (GWM pattern). Dice riders (+1d6) read as 2014 — prefer PB.
- Forced movement: push 10 ft / knock Prone on hit, often gated by a
  save or size (Crusher, Tavern Brawler patterns).
- Debuff riders: reduce Speed, no Reactions until next turn
  (Slasher, Sentinel patterns).

**Defensive bricks:**
- Reactions that protect: impose Disadvantage (Protection), halve or
  reduce damage (Interception), retaliate (Sentinel).
- Conditional resistance: against one damage type, in one stance —
  permanent broad resistance is Epic Boon territory (Energy Resistance).
- Temp HP pulses: gain PB temp HP when [condition] — fine on General,
  generous on Origin.
- Rerolls: reroll a failed save 1/Long Rest; reroll 1s on specific dice
  (Healer, Great Weapon Fighting patterns).

**Action economy bricks — DANGER ZONE.**
Action economy is the most explosive currency in 5e. Official feats
spend it only with triggers attached:
- Bonus Action attack **only after a crit or a kill** (GWM's Hew).
- Bonus Action utility (Disengage — Boon of Speed; Study — Keen Mind)
  is safe; Bonus Action *attacks* without a trigger are not.
- Extra Reactions, extra turns, free Actions: **never** below Epic
  Boon, and even there avoid loops (see balance-and-anti-patterns.md).

**Weapon Mastery interactions (new 2024 subsystem):**
- Weapon Mastery properties (Sap, Vex, Topple, Cleave, Graze, Nick,
  Push, Slow) are gated behind class features. A feat may *extend*
  mastery (use a second property, swap faster) but should not *grant*
  mastery wholesale to classes that lack it — that walks on the Fighter.
- The official lever: Martial Weapon Training grants proficiency, not
  mastery. Respect that line.

## Exploration Bricks

- **Movement:** +10 ft Speed (Speedy pattern — General-grade with ASI),
  Climb or Swim Speed equal to Speed, ignore Difficult Terrain when
  Dashing (Speedy), standing jump distance.
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft or +60 ft extension, Blindsight 10 ft
  (Blind Fighting — note how short the range is). Truesight is
  exclusively Epic (Boon of Truesight).
- **Survival texture:** forage for PB creatures, never lost by
  nonmagical means, weather immunity — cheap bricks, good Origin
  material, fine as the *third* brick of a General feat.
- **Tools and crafting:** proficiency + crafting discounts/speed
  (Crafter pattern), portable workshop tricks.

## Social Bricks

- **Proficiency grants:** 1–3 skills/tools (Skilled = 3 is the budget
  ceiling). Expertise on a feat is rare officially (Skill Expert 2014;
  in 2024, Boon of Skill) — treat Expertise as 2 bricks.
- **Conditional Advantage:** on Influence checks against [a faction /
  creatures you share a language with / people you've dined with] —
  the condition is the balance lever.
- **Reading people:** learn one of a creature's Bonds/Flaws after 1
  minute of talk; Insight vs Deception riders.
- **Narrative contacts:** "you know someone in every port" — zero math,
  high table value, classic Origin material.
- **Heroic Inspiration engines:** grant it to allies after a rest
  (Musician), gain it when you roll a 1 (Human's racial trait — don't
  duplicate), gain it on a long social ritual.

## Magic Bricks

- **The Magic Initiate chassis:** 2 cantrips + 1 level-1 spell from one
  list, 1 free cast/Long Rest, re-castable with slots. Reskin by list,
  theme-restrict the choices ("only fire spells") for a tighter feat.
- **The touched chassis (Fey-Touched / Shadow-Touched):** +1 mental ASI
  + Misty Step-grade signature spell + 1 thematic level-1 spell, each
  free 1/Long Rest. The General-feat way to grant a level-2 spell.
- **Concentration support:** Advantage on Concentration saves (War
  Caster) — do not stack further Concentration protection on top.
- **Ritual access:** the Ritual Caster pattern — utility spellcasting
  with a time cost, almost free in balance terms.
- **Component waivers:** cast with hands full / observed casting
  appears mundane — texture bricks, cheap.

## Scaling Knobs — One Concept, Three Power Levels

To grow a brick from one category to the next, turn exactly one knob:
**add the ASI and remove a restriction** (Origin → General), or
**remove the resource limit / make it permanent** (General → Epic Boon).

| Concept | Origin version | General version | Epic Boon version |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shadow affinity | Advantage on Stealth in Dim Light, 1 free *Minor Illusion* | +1 Dex/Cha, cast *Invisibility* on self 1/Long Rest | Invisibility + resistance whenever in Dim Light or Darkness (= Boon of the Night Spirit) |
| Battle luck | Reroll one damage die per turn (= Savage Attacker) | +1 Str/Dex, turn one miss into a reroll PB/Long Rest | Once per turn, turn a miss into a hit (= Boon of Combat Prowess) |
| Skill mastery | 3 proficiencies (= Skilled) | +1 any, 1 proficiency + 1 Expertise (= Skill Expert chassis) | All skills + 1 Expertise (= Boon of Skill) |

If you cannot place your concept on this ladder, you do not yet know
what the feat's signature is — go back to concept before writing text.
