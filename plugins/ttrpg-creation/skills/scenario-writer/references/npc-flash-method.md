# NPC Flash Method

Quick NPC creation for scenario-scale characters. Use this for NPCs who appear briefly or serve functional roles. For major recurring NPCs, invoke the **npc-creator** skill instead.

## The Flash Profile Format

```markdown
### [Name], [Role]
**Appearance**: [One striking visual detail]  
**Manner**: [One behavioral trait for roleplay]  
**Secret/Motivation**: [What drives them or what they hide]  
**Stats**: [Reference or inline stats]
```

**Total: 4 lines maximum.**

## Guidelines

### Appearance (One Detail)
Choose the most memorable visual element—what players will remember.

**Strong choices:**
- "A purple scar bisects her left cheek"
- "Missing three fingers on his sword hand"
- "Wears her dead husband's military coat, too large for her"
- "Teeth filed to points"

**Weak choices:**
- "Average height, brown hair, brown eyes" (generic)
- "Wears leather armor and carries a sword" (equipment, not character)
- "Looks tired" (temporary state, not defining trait)

### Manner (One Trait)
Give the GM something to *do* at the table.

**Strong choices:**
- "Never makes eye contact, always looking past you"
- "Speaks in questions, even when stating facts"
- "Laughs before delivering bad news"
- "Touches everything within reach while talking"

**Weak choices:**
- "Seems suspicious" (player interpretation, not behavior)
- "Is loyal to the king" (motivation, not manner)
- "Speaks with an accent" (which accent? doing what?)

### Secret/Motivation (One Line)
What the GM needs to know to play this NPC authentically.

**For antagonists:** What they want and why
**For allies:** What they need or fear
**For neutral NPCs:** What they're hiding or protecting

### Stats (Reference or Inline)

**Reference format** (preferred for standard creatures):
```
**Stats**: Guard Captain (MM p.X) or Veteran stat block
```

**Inline format** (for quick reference):
```
**Stats**: AC 14, HP 22, +4 to hit, 1d8+2 damage
```

**Extended inline** (if combat likely):
```
**Stats**: AC 15 (chain shirt), HP 32, Spd 30ft; +5 Longsword (1d8+3); Perception +4
```

## Flash Profiles by Archetype

### Authority Figures

```markdown
### Captain Hask, City Watch Commander
**Appearance**: Greying beard trimmed military-short, old burn scar on neck  
**Manner**: Finishes your sentences, always impatient  
**Secret**: Knows the mayor is corrupt but needs evidence before acting  
**Stats**: Veteran (MM p.350)
```

```markdown
### Madame Lirael, House Proprietor
**Appearance**: Dark hair in elaborate braids, amber eyes that see through pretense, timeless elegance  
**Manner**: Speaks softly, finishes others' sentences, perfect composure that cracks only when discussing Cirelle  
**Secret**: Helped establish the original binding, has maintained the House for centuries to monitor the entity  
**Stats**: See Technical Appendix (CR 7) or Noble with Insight +8, Persuasion +9
```

```markdown
### Bishop Aldric Morvaine, Corrupt Church Leader
**Appearance**: Rotund man in his sixties, carefully maintained white hair, expensive ecclesiastical robes, jeweled rings on every finger  
**Manner**: Warm, practiced charm that never reaches calculating eyes, quotes scripture mid-sentence, laughs too readily  
**Secret**: Made a deal with the entity, offering to help it awaken in exchange for power, terrified of exposure  
**Stats**: Priest (MM p.348) with Deception +6, or see Technical Appendix (CR 5)
```

```markdown
**Manner**: Speaks in the third person, never sits still  
**Secret**: Works for three different factions, sells information to all

```

```markdown
### Echo Blackwood, Dream-Touched Ally
**Appearance**: Looks like they haven't slept in weeks, dark circles beneath eyes, hands that shake slightly  
**Manner**: Recognizes purpose before words are spoken, speaks in fragments, understands corruption from personal experience  
**Secret**: Fights corruption from the inside, helped Cirelle hide, knows about entity's influence  
**Stats**: Commoner (MM p.345), but with Insight +4, Investigation +3, knows *detect magic* as ritual
```

```markdown
### Verity "Coins" Thrace, Information Broker
**Appearance**: Halfling with copper hair, ageless face, constantly moving, shop is a maze of curiosities  
**Manner**: Speaks in questions even when stating facts, never sits still, trades information for information or coin  
**Secret**: Works for multiple factions, has heard whispers about "confession that could topple empires"  
**Stats**: Spy (MM p.349), but with Insight +6, Investigation +5
```

### Antagonist Minions

```markdown
### Scar, Cult Enforcer
**Appearance**: Ritual brands covering both forearms  
**Manner**: Smiles constantly, even while threatening  
**Secret**: True believer, welcomes death in service to the cause  
**Stats**: Thug (MM p.350), +2 damage when flanking
```

```markdown
### The Shadow, Entity-Touched Assassin
**Appearance**: Figure more corrupted than human, shadows seem to cling to them, eyes that reflect nothing  
**Manner**: Speaks in whispers if at all, attacks without warning, shows no emotion, cannot be reasoned with  
**Secret**: Humanity already lost to entity's influence, will die to complete mission  
**Stats**: See Technical Appendix (CR 3, custom stat block with Shadow Step ability)
```

```markdown

**Manner**: Speaks softly, makes you lean in to hear  
**Secret**: Embezzling from the boss, planning to flee soon  
**Stats**: Bandit Captain (MM p.344)
```

## When to Upgrade to npc-creator

Invoke the full **npc-creator** skill when:

- NPC will appear in 3+ scenes
- NPC has combat role requiring full stat block
- NPC's backstory directly impacts plot
- NPC may become recurring across sessions
- Players have shown unexpected interest in the character
- NPC needs extensive portrayal guidance (voice, multiple tells, etc.)

## Quick Reference Table

| Role | Likely Stats | Key Question |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Authority | Noble, Knight, Veteran | What power do they wield? |
| Informant | Spy, Commoner | What do they know and want? |
| Merchant | Commoner, Noble | What do they sell and fear? |
| Criminal | Thug, Bandit, Assassin | What lines won't they cross? |
| Clergy | Acolyte, Priest | What do they believe vs. practice? |
| Scholar | Commoner, Mage | What knowledge are they hoarding? |
| Guard | Guard, Veteran | What are they protecting and why? |
| Victim | Commoner | What did they see, and what stops them from talking? |
