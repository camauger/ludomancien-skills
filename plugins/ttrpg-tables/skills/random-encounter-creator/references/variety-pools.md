# Variety Pools

Reference lists to ensure diversity across large encounter tables. Rotate through these to avoid repetition.

## NPC Archetypes

### Lower Class
- Beggar (blind, crippled, child, veteran, fraudulent)
- Street vendor (food, trinkets, flowers, news sheets)
- Laborer (dock worker, construction, grave digger, sewer)
- Servant (household, tavern, stable, temple)
- Prostitute (desperate, professional, retired, infected)
- Thief (pickpocket, burglar, fence, lookout)
- Urchin (orphan, runaway, gang member, messenger)

### Middle Class
- Merchant (cloth, spices, weapons, curiosities, livestock)
- Artisan (blacksmith, carpenter, tailor, jeweler, scribe)
- Innkeeper (friendly, suspicious, corrupt, haunted)
- Guard (honest, corrupt, lazy, zealous, rookie)
- Clerk (tax collector, notary, guild secretary)
- Healer (herbalist, barber-surgeon, midwife, quack)
- Entertainer (musician, actor, storyteller, fortune teller)

### Upper Class
- Noble (minor house, major house, disgraced, ambitious)
- Priest (devout, corrupt, doubting, fanatical)
- Officer (guard captain, military, naval, retired)
- Scholar (historian, naturalist, occultist, tutor)
- Magistrate (fair, corrupt, incompetent, cruel)
- Merchant prince (shipping, banking, guild master)

### Outsiders
- Foreigner (refugee, diplomat, spy, tourist, exile)
- Sailor (captain, first mate, deckhand, retired)
- Mercenary (veteran, recruit, deserter, famous)
- Hermit (mad, wise, dangerous, prophetic)
- Pilgrim (devout, skeptic, dying, secret sinner)

## Creatures & Animals

- Rat (single, swarm, giant, diseased, intelligent)
- Cat (stray, hunting, pampered, omen)
- Dog (stray, guard, fighting, loyal, rabid)
- Horse (draft, riding, carriage, abandoned)
- Crow/Raven (single, murder, trained, omen)
- Pigeon (flock, messenger, pest)

### Uncommon
- Snake (escaped, wild, venomous, symbolic)
- Spider (large, swarm, exotic, web-spinner)
- Owl (hunting, watching, omen, familiar)
- Fox (urban, rabid, clever, magical)
- Pig (market, escaped, wild, truffle-hunter)

### Supernatural
- Ghost (restless, vengeful, lost, protective)
- Shade (fragment, echo, warning)
- Vermin swarm (unnatural, coordinated, diseased)
- Animated object (servant, trap, art piece)
- Familiar (obvious, disguised, abandoned)

### Valuable
- Purse (full, empty, trapped, marked)
- Jewelry (ring, necklace, brooch, stolen)
- Document (deed, will, love letter, blackmail)
- Key (ordinary, ornate, to nowhere, many locks)
- Weapon (fine, hidden, cursed, famous)

### Mundane
- Cart (overturned, blocking, abandoned, delivery)
- Barrel (wine, fish, bodies, hiding)
- Crate (ordinary, contraband, trapped, empty)
- Lantern (broken, lit, magical, signal)
- Rope (hanging, trap, escape, binding)

### Disturbing
- Blood (fresh, dried, trail, pool)
- Tooth/Bone (human, animal, pile, necklace)
- Body part (finger, hand, head, wrapped)
- Doll (child's, effigy, moving, watching)
- Mirror (cracked, covered, shows wrong reflection)

### Written
- Letter (love, threat, confession, coded)
- List (names, debts, targets, groceries)
- Book (journal, spellbook, ledger, banned)
- Poster (wanted, event, propaganda, warning)

## Smells

### Pleasant
- Baking (bread, pastries, meat pies)
- Flowers (market, funeral, perfume, rot)
- Incense (temple, shop, concealing)
- Spices (market, kitchen, exotic)
- Rain (fresh, coming, past)

### Unpleasant
- Sewage (backed up, nearby, hidden)
- Rot (food, bodies, wood)

## Weather & Atmosphere

### Common
- Fog (thick, patchy, rolling in, lifting)
- Rain (drizzle, downpour, stopping, threatening)
- Wind (gentle, gusting, howling, still)
- Heat (oppressive, muggy, dry, haze)

### Unusual
- Unseasonable (wrong heat, wrong cold)
- Localized (one street, one building)
- Changing (sudden shift, rapid cycles)
- Static (unchanging for days, wrong)

### Ominous
- Eclipse (partial, total, unexpected)
- Blood moon (prophecy, magic, fear)
- Green sky (toxic, magical, warning)
- No stars (covered, vanished, wrong)
